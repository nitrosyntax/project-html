Peanut Butter & Banana Delight

Instructions

Ingredients:

  • Bananas - 1
  • Peanut butter - 2 tbsp
  • Bread - 2 slices(any kind)
  • Butter (at room temperature; to cook the sandwich)

Recipe:

  1. Cut up the banana into equally think slices.
  2. Spread a tablespoon of the peanut butter on each slice of the bread.
  3. Place the banana slices on one of the bread slices and place the other bread slice (with the peanut butter covered side facing the bananas); press firmly.
  4. Apply a little bit of the butter to one side of the sandwich and cook over medium heat in a pan; turn it over and cook the other side until golden brown (2-3 minutes per side).
  5. Plate it and serve hot!!
