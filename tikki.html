Chatpata Aloo Tikki

Follow these simple instructions to fulfil your craving!

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes - 3 (boiled)
  • Chilli - 1
  • Ginger garlic paste - 1 tsp
  • Turmeric - 1/4 tsp
  • Red chilli powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Cumin powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Aamchur/dry mango powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Chaat masala - 1/2 tsp
  • Salt - 1/2 tsp
  • Pudina - 2 tbsp (chopped)
  • Coriander - 2 tbsp (chopped)
  • Corn flour - 2 tbsp
  • Oil (for frying)

Recipe:

  1. Grate the boiled potato into a large mixing bowl.
  2. Add the chilli, ginger garlic paste, tumeric and chilli powder, along with the cumin powder, aamchur, chaat masala and salt.
  3. Mix well.
  4. Now add the corn flour and mix it, forming a soft dough. (Poha powder or rice flour can be used in place of the corn flour to absorb moisture)
  5. Grease your hands with a little bit of the oil and prepare equally sized balls for the tikki, then flatten it slightly.
  6. Shallow fry these in hot oil, or alternatively roast or deep fry.
  7. Cook both sides until evenly golden brown.
  8. Serve hot with green chutney or tamarind chutney.

    9. You can use these tikkis to make aloo tikki chaat as well!
