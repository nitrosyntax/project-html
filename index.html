Happiness in a glass...

How to find this happiness?

Just follow these simple instructions:

Ingredients:

  • Chilled milk - 1 cup (180-200 mL)
  • Water - 1/4 cup (45-50 mL)
  • Instant coffee powder - 1 tbsp
  • Sugar - 1 tbsp (alter as per taste)
  • Ice cubes - 6-8

Recipe:

  1. In a small pan, pour the water and bring it to a boil.
  2. Take the pan off the stove and add the coffee powder, sugar and mix well.
  3. Whisk the mixture with a fork or a whisk, or use an electric whisk to do the same, until the mixture forms soft peaks.


  4. Put the ice cubes in a mug and pour the milk into it.
  5. Softly pour the coffee mixture on top of this. This can be done with a spoon as well.
  6. Click a few pictures, then give it a mix and enjoy!!
